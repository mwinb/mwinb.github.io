About Me:

I was born in Redding, CA. Gruaduated Shasta High School in 2009. Spent 3 months in Maui with a friend after graduation where I decided to join the U.S. Navy. I graduated from U.S. Navy Hospital Corps School in 2010, after which I spent 4 years at First Marine Infantry Division on Camp Pendleton near San Clemente, California. I deployed as a line Corpsman (Combat Medic) in 2011, with 1st Battalion 5th Marine Regiment, to Southern Helmand Province in Afghanistan. Upon returning from deployment I spent two years as the Assitant Director for the Combat Trauma course at First Marine Division. After separating from the military, I graduated from Cuesta Community College with an Associates Degree in Computer Science. I am currently continuing my education at Oregon State University - Corvalis.
